About Swiftel Fibre

Your trusted partner for high-speed internet connectivity in Kasarani.

Our Story

Swiftel Fibre was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the digital divide in Kenya by providing unparalleled internet speed and reliability. Our journey began with a commitment to bring world-class fibre optic technology directly to homes and businesses, ensuring everyone has access to the power of the internet. We officially launched our operations in March 2021, and since then, we have been dedicated to enhancing digital connectivity. We understand the critical role that robust internet plays in modern life, whether it's for learning, working, entertainment, or running a thriving business.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the internet generation through seamless, high-speed connectivity. We are dedicated to delivering innovative fibre optic solutions that enhance productivity, foster communication, and unlock new opportunities for individuals and enterprises across our service areas. We strive to be more than just an ISP; we aim to be a partner in your digital growth.
