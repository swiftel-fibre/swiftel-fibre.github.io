Our Comprehensive Services

Solutions Tailored to Your Needs

Wholesale Internet Capacity

We provide high-capacity, dedicated internet bandwidth for Internet Service Providers (ISPs) and large enterprises, ensuring robust and reliable connectivity.

Key Features:

  • Dedicated fiber and wireless backbone solutions
  • Scalable capacity (from Mbps to Gbps) to grow with your demand
  • 24/7 network monitoring and SLA-backed uptime guarantees
  • Peering with local and international exchanges for optimized routing

Use Cases:

  • Reselling internet to end customers
  • Backbone for branch office connectivity across locations
  • Efficient content delivery and streaming services

Hotspot Solutions

Complete design, setup, and management of secure and branded hotspot networks for public and commercial spaces, enhancing visitor experience and generating revenue.

Key Features:

  • Customizable captive portal for user login and branding
  • Advanced bandwidth control and user session management
  • Comprehensive analytics dashboard (usage, devices, sessions)
  • Flexible voucher-based or pay-per-use billing models

Use Cases:

  • Shopping malls, cafes, campuses, and event venues
  • Marketing through integrated SMS/WhatsApp login
  • Revenue generation through convenient access billing
