About Swiftel Fibre

Founded in 2022, Swiftel Fibre is a leading Internet Service Provider dedicated to delivering high-speed, reliable internet to both residential homes and businesses. We are proud to serve the communities of Kasarani and Njiru constituencies in Nairobi County, empowering the next generation with seamless connectivity.

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing affordable and efficient internet solutions. Whether you're streaming, gaming, or running a business, Swiftel Fibre has a plan tailored to meet your needs.
